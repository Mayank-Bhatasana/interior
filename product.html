About This Chair

Every Walnut Lounge Chair begins as carefully selected boards from American Black Walnut — one of the most prized hardwoods in the world. Our master craftsmen hand-shape each component, paying special attention to the grain flow and figure that make every chair unique.

The joinery uses traditional mortise-and-tenon construction reinforced with modern adhesives, ensuring the chair will last for generations. The finish is a hand-rubbed oil that enhances the wood's natural beauty while providing protection.

Who We Are

Born from a Love of Wood and Craft

UrbanNest Interior began in 2010 when founder Marcus Wood left a career in architecture to pursue his passion for furniture making. Starting with a small workshop in Vermont, he began crafting pieces that combined the precision of a designer with the soul of an artisan.

Today, our team of 45 skilled craftspeople, designers, and installers works out of our 12,000 sq ft studio, producing hundreds of custom and ready-made pieces every year. We ship to customers across the United States and Canada.

Our philosophy is simple: great furniture should last a lifetime. We use only sustainably sourced hardwoods, proven joinery techniques, and finishes that protect without masking nature's beauty.
